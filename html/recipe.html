People favorite

1. Croissant

This croissant is a masterpiece of patience and precision — 27 delicate layers of buttery dough, folded and baked to golden perfection. Each bite is crisp and flaky on the outside, soft and airy on the inside, with a rich aroma that only real butter can bring. It’s the kind of croissant that makes mornings feel special and every bite worth savoring.

2. Chocolate, Matcha, Creme Brulee macaron

These macarons are a delightful fusion of flavors that bring together the best of three worlds. The rich chocolate shell pairs beautifully with the earthy aroma of matcha, while a creamy crème brûlée filling adds a touch of indulgent sweetness. Each bite offers a perfect balance of crisp, chewy, and creamy textures — a little luxury you’ll want to savor slowly. Perfect for special occasions or when you simply want to treat yourself.

3. Butter Scone

This scone is all about pure, buttery perfection. Made with 84% Canadian butter, it has a rich, creamy flavor and a delicate, flaky texture that melts in your mouth. Lightly crisp on the outside and tender inside, it’s a simple yet luxurious treat that pairs beautifully with tea or coffee. Enjoy it warm for the ultimate buttery experience
